Yunus (Jonah)

Qur’an • Al-Anbiya 21:87–88

لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا أَنتَ سُبْحَانَكَ إِنِّي كُنتُ مِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ

Transliteration

Lā ilāha illā anta subḥānaka innī kuntu minaẓ-ẓālimīn.

English

There is no deity except You; exalted are You. Indeed, I have been of the wrongdoers.

Türkçe

Senden başka ilah yoktur. Seni noksan sıfatlardan tenzih ederim. Gerçekten ben zalimlerden oldum.

Tags
tawbah distress forgiveness hope
Reflection & Context ⌄

This prayer is recited in moments of distress and returning to Allah. It carries humility and recognition of one’s shortcomings, opening a path to mercy and relief.

Ayyub (Job)

Qur’an • Al-Anbiya 21:83

أَنِّي مَسَّنِيَ الضُّرُّ وَأَنتَ أَرْحَمُ الرَّاحِمِينَ

Transliteration

Annī massaniyaḍ-ḍurru wa anta arḥamu r-rāḥimīn.

English

Indeed, adversity has touched me, and You are the Most Merciful of the merciful.

Türkçe

Bana gerçekten sıkıntı dokundu. Sen merhametlilerin en merhametlisisin.

Tags
healing patience mercy trial
Reflection & Context ⌄

A prayer of dignified vulnerability: naming the hardship without despair, and affirming Allah’s mercy above all means.

Zakariya (Zechariah)

Qur’an • Maryam 19:4–6

رَبِّ إِنِّي وَهَنَ الْعَظْمُ مِنِّي وَاشْتَعَلَ الرَّأْسُ شَيْبًا وَلَمْ أَكُن بِدُعَائِكَ رَبِّ شَقِيًّا

Transliteration

Rabbi innī wahanal-ʿaẓmu minnī washtaʿala r-ra'su shaybā, wa lam akun biduʿā'ika rabbi shaqiyyā.

English

My Lord, my bones have weakened and my head has filled with gray, yet I have never been disappointed in my supplication to You.

Türkçe

Rabbim! Kemiklerim zayıfladı, başım ihtiyarlıkla ağardı. Ama sana duamda Rabbim, hiç bedbaht olmadım.

Tags
family hope legacy trust
Reflection & Context ⌄

A prayer that blends honesty about weakness with a long memory of answered dua — a soft invitation to keep asking with hope.
